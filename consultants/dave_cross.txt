Dave has been using Perl since 1996. In 1998, he started the
<a href="http://london.pm.org/">London Perl Mongers</a>, the first Perl
users' group outside of North America. He is still heavily involved with the
Perl community and is one of the team running 
<a href="http://blogs.perl.org/">blogs.perl.org</a>.
Dave is the author of
<a href="http://manning.com/cross/">Data Munging with Perl</a> and a
co-author of <a href="http://shop.oreilly.com/product/9780596004767.do">Perl
Template Toolkit</a>. He has been training since 2000 and is a regular speaker
at both Perl and Open Source conferences. Dave blogs about Perl at
<a href="http://perlhacks.com/">Perl Hacks</a> and he offers training and
consultancy through
<a href="http://mag-sol.com/">Magnum Solutions</a> and
<a href="http://www.allaroundtheworld.fr">All Around The World</a>.
